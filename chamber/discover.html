Discover some amazing places

Makola Market

Kojokrom Street, Accra

A bustling market in the heart of Accra, known for its wide variety of goods and vibrant atmosphere.

Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum

High Street, Accra

The final resting place of Ghana's first president, Kwame Nkrumah, a symbol of Pan-Africanism.

National Museum of Ghana

Barnes Road, Accra

Showcases Ghana's rich cultural heritage through artifacts, exhibits, and art.

Independence Square

Osu, Accra

A significant landmark where Ghana declared its independence, featuring the Black Star Gate.

Labadi Beach

Labadi, Accra

A popular beach resort offering relaxation, entertainment, and stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean.

Aburi Botanical Gardens

Aburi, Eastern Region (near Accra)

Beautiful gardens with diverse flora and fauna, offering a peaceful escape from the city.

Art Centre

High Street, Accra

A hub for local artists and craftspeople, showcasing and selling traditional Ghanaian arts and crafts.

Osu Castle (Christiansborg Castle)

Osu, Accra

A historic castle with a rich and complex past, serving as a former slave trading post and presidential residence.

Accra - Ghana
